Quick Guide
Credits

Credits are the currency of learning. Each credit equals 30 minutes of mentoring time. Learners spend them to book sessions, and mentors earn them by teaching. Everyone starts with 5 credits.

Rating

At the end of each session, learners rate their mentors. Your rating helps others find the best mentors for them.
