Credits

Credits are the currency of learning. Each credit equals 30 minutes of mentoring time, learners spend them to book sessions, and mentors earn them by teaching. Everyone starts with 5 credits, creating a fair cycle of growth and sharing.

Rating

At the end of each session, learners rate their mentors, showing how satisfied they were with the guidance received.
